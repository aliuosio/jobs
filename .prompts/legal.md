- Job Description
- Applicant Cover Letter

  - Criteria Alignment: (0–30)

  - Bias Risk Mitigation: (0–20)
  - Role Level & Structure Consistency: (0–15)
  - Equity Safeguard Adjustment: (-10 to 0)
- Objective Match Summary (criteria-based)
- Fairness & Bias Risk Assessment
- Required Follow-Ups or Documentation
- HR Recommendation:
  - Process can proceed
  - Proceed with conditions
  - Pause for audit/review

IMPORTANT:
Do not assess technical excellence.
Ensure all scores are auditable, explainable, and legally defensible.
